I've decided to host a virtual scavenger hunt for my birthday since we can't be together. Zoom meetings get tiring so this hunt is done on your own time and pace. You'll be sharing pics which allows everyone to take part in this hunt. The goal is fun and not meant to add any stress to your life.

Overview
  • Create an account to access the hunt. (see email for details)
  • The timer starts once you login with your account.
  • The site will track your progress, upload pics, upload text and keep score.
  • Contact Matt if you have login issues.
  • The hunt officially ends on Jan 17th, 11:59PM eastern. progress, upload pics and text.
  • Winners get bragging rights.
Rules
  1. Have fun and be creative!
  2. Completing tasks for the hunt is on the honor system. No one is verifying the uploads.
  3. Be respectful of others.
  4. Each task completed will add points to your account. Not all tasks need completed
  5. Scores will be shown in your account as well as the main page.
  6. You will be timed on completing the hunt. The timer ends when you click the Finalize button.
  7. If there is a tie with point scores, the winner is determined by having a shorter time to complete the list.
About the site

All content you share will be visible to anyone with a link to this page. All content becomes Matt's property. This site will be taken down after the hunt is over.
